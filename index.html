But I don't have any of the above apps ... what do you suggest?

You can download any Podcast player for your phone or computer, as appropriate for your Operating System (Windows, Mac or Linux) and use the following link to subscribe.

https://knowledgeablyyours.github.io/podcast.xml

Typically, most podcast apps will have an "Add Feed" button. Once you click this, all you need to do is copy-paste the above link to this application and voila - you are set!
